🥒 Cucumber De-Puff Hack
Blend or grate a fresh cucumber into a smooth paste. Place it into a clean empty lipstick container (or another small clean container) and chill it in the fridge until cold. Apply gently under your eyes to help reduce puffiness and leave your eyes feeling refreshed. 💕
🍌 Banana Glow Mask
Mash half of a ripe banana with a fork until smooth. Mix in one teaspoon of plain yogurt or honey for extra moisture if you like. Apply the mask to clean skin and leave it on for 10–15 minutes before rinsing with lukewarm water. 💕 Bananas contain vitamins and natural sugars that can help your skin feel soft and hydrated, while yogurt or honey can add extra moisture. If you have sensitive skin, test a small amount on your wrist first before applying it to your face.

✨ Flyaway Hair Tamer ✨
Lightly spray a small fluffy makeup brush (like a blush brush) with a little hairspray. Gently brush it over the top of your hair where you have flyaways or little wispy hairs. 💕 This helps smooth everything down without making your hair look stiff or greasy. Because the hairspray is applied with a brush instead of directly onto your hair, it gives a much more natural finish while helping your hairstyle stay neat all day.
