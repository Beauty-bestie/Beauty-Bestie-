🥒 Cucumber Eye De-Puff Hack
Take a fresh cucumber and blend or grate it into a soft paste. Put it into a clean lipstick case or small container and chill it in the fridge. When cold, apply it gently under your eyes. The cooling effect reduces puffiness and helps refresh tired eyes 💕
